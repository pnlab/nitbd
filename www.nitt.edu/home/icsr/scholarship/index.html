    Sudeep Kandasami Scholarship

    Sudeep Kandasami (25th April 1987 to 14th October 2010) was born in Thadepalligudem, AndhraPradesh. He completed his schooling from DAV, BHEL, Ranipet with top scores excelling not only in academics but also earning a black belt in karate and representing his school in football, athletics and other sporting activities. He continued his education at the National Institute of Technology, Tiruchirapalli from the summer of 2004 and earned distinction and passed out in the summer of 2008. It was here that he found his passion for electronics and continued to excel at all his ventures, be it, earning grades, winning technical competitions or teaching fellow students. Sudeep was then recruited by nVidia, the world's leading graphic processor maker and continued his pursuit of technical excellence at nVidia. He showed remarkable talent, creativity and passion at nVidia tackling challenges that were beyond the scope of freshers. Sadly the journey ended too soon, much before it even started and what looked like a very promising life was over.
    Sudeep was part of a very close knit family. His parents and his sister now live in Bangalore. He was an integral part of his family and our heart felt condolences are always with them. We, his friends have seen the passion Sudeep had for academics and would like to use this opportunity to facilitate and aid passionate students in their aim to excel in the field of electronics. Hence we have devised this scholarship so that it may pave the way for many such students in the future.

    a) Name : Sudeep Kandasami Scholarship
    b) Scholarship: Rs 15,000 would be payable towards 5th semester fee of a candidate, who was the topper of 3rd semester ECE department. The first annual scholarship shall be disbursed in 2013 for the ECE - 3rd semester topper of 2012.
    c) Process: The HOD will forward the list of eligible student(s) to RECAL Foundation to complete the disbursements in time

    BHARTI SCHOLARSHIP

    Bharti Foundation was established in the year 2000, with the vision 'To help underprivileged children and young people of our country realise their potential.' Bharti Foundation's mission is to create and support programs that bring about sustainable changes through education, use of technology, and information & best practices sharing.

    Bharti Foundation has set itself the goals of improving accessibility and quality of education at the school level for underprivileged children, and to provide education and training opportunities to youth.

    Under its school-level programs, Bharti Foundation has been focusing on improving the learning levels of children. It has programs in the areas of computers in education, library and reading programs, mid-day meals, among other initiatives. For youth, in addition to supporting institutes of higher education, Bharti Foundation has set up a Bharti Scholarship Scheme and has also been encouraging entrepreneurship. It has instituted awards and fellowships to motivate young people in achieving their potential.

    SC/ST scholarship

    The Government of India has introduced a special scheme of scholarship to SC/ST students who have secured admission in the Institute during the Academic year 2007 – 2008 on the basis of their ranks obtained by them in AIEEE. Only top 10 rank holders under SC/ST category and whose family income (Parental income) is less than Rs.2 lakhs per annum alone are eligible as per the Govt. norms.
